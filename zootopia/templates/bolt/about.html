Who we are

We're the Bolt animal service center and we've been here for animals since 2021. We're the famous animal welfare charity in Glasgow, with the primary focus of rescuing, rehabilitating and rehoming or releasing animals across Scotland. Every day, with the help of our people and our generous supporters, we take steps to achieve our mission.
